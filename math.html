Key Results

Monotonic Increase

G(n) < G(n+1)   for all n ≥ 1

The Amundson sequence is strictly increasing. Each term exceeds the previous, approaching the limit from below without ever reaching it.

Ratio Convergence

lim G(n+1)/G(n) = e ≈ 2.71828

The ratio of consecutive terms converges to Euler's number. This connects the Amundson sequence to the natural exponential and continuous growth.

e-Limit Refinement

n / (1 + 1/n)^n = n/e + 1/(2e) + O(1/n)

The classical limit (1+1/n)^n approaches e, but the refinement reveals an irreducible correction term that never vanishes.

The Irreducible Gap

1/(2e) ≈ 0.18393972

The constant 1/(2e) represents the irreducible gap between the finite and the infinite. No amount of iteration eliminates it. It is the signature of discreteness in a continuous limit.

Application

Coherence Function

The Amundson sequence powers BlackRoad's coherence function. Under contradiction, coherence amplifies rather than degrades. The 1/(2e) gap ensures the system remains bounded while preserving sensitivity to perturbation.

K(t) = C(t) · eλ|δ|

where C(t) is base coherence, λ is the amplification rate, and δ is contradiction magnitude.
